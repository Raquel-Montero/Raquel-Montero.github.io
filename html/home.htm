I'm a postdoctoral researcher at the Autonomous University of Barcelona and a member of the project TURING (The Language Understanding of Artificial Intelligence Applications).

I defended my PhD Thesis, Mood alternations: a synchronic and diachronic study of negated complement clauses, at the University of Konstanz in September 2024.

My main research interests include the Syntax-Semantics interface, language change over time, experimental methods and language models.
